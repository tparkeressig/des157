4. How do your users think your interactive projects should work and what are their experience levels with the type of media you are creating?
I assume the users will be expecting a much more linear and structured experience. The average user in our class or at the ISEA conference will have quite a fair amount of experience with digital interfaces. However, they may be new or somewhat new to Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality interfaces.
